About Us

Team VeerRacerss Electric is a team of 25 undergraduate engineering students and automobile enthusiasts from Veer Surendra Sai University of Technology in Burla, Odisha. We design and fabricate a Formula-Student Prototype vehicle which is judged in the areas of Engineering Design, Cost effectiveness, Marketability& Performance. The vehicle is made to be in line with the stringent norms laid down by the events.

Our Journey




  • 2014

    Beginnings!

    Only a discerning eyes can value eminent things, and this vision which led to dawn of this journey was started by Avinash Sharma, who regardless of any perception moved ahead in this path.

  • 2015

    An CV Team is Born!

    "Individually, we are one drop.
    Together, we are an ocean."
    The small lake of dreams which Avinash created, attracted many enthusiastic brook which together created this club "VEER RACERS" and this team work regulated the flow of this beautiful journey.

  • 2020

    Shifting to EV!

    Advancement is the aftermath of innovation, creativity and change. And this shift towards EV provided VEER RACERS an alligned vision with development in today's era.

  • 2023

    Our First Offline Event!

    Coming together is a beginning, staying together is progress, and working together is success. And we are going to organize our first offline event in 2023.
    Still working together! Still dreaming to achieve more and we will achieve more because 'we are race-heads born to hustle and grin!'
